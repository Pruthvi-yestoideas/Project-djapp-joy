How to Solve the "Tracking NO" Column Issue in Data File
If Using Microsoft Excel:
  • Launch Excel and open a blank workbook.
  • Navigate to the 'Data' tab and select 'From Text/CSV'.
  • Locate and open the CSV file.
  • In the dialog that appears, find the "Tracking NO" column.
  • Change its data type from 'General' or 'Number' to 'Text'.
  • Click 'Load' to finish importing the data.

If Using LibreOffice Calc:
  • Open LibreOffice Calc.
  • Navigate to 'File' > 'Open' and select the CSV file.
  • The Text Import dialog will appear.
  • For the "Tracking NO" column, change its format to 'Text'.
  • Click 'OK' to import the data.
How to Load your Balance with Crypto

Sending Tether (USDT) is a straightforward process, but it's crucial to be careful at every step to ensure the transaction goes smoothly. Below are the steps to send USDT from a typical cryptocurrency wallet. Please note that the steps may vary slightly depending on the wallet you are using.

Pre-requisites

  • Cryptocurrency Wallet: Ensure you have a cryptocurrency wallet that supports USDT. Examples include Trust Wallet, MetaMask, and MyEtherWallet.

  • For wallet setup and USDT acquisition, please check Coinbase guide.

  • USDT Balance: Make sure you have enough USDT in your wallet to send. Also, account for any transaction fees.

  • Receiver's Address: Have the recipient's USDT address handy. It's essential to get the address correct to avoid losing funds.

Steps to Send USDT featuring CoinBase

Step 1: Get the Receiver Address

  • Navigate to "Add Balance" to get our Wallet Address
Step 2: Initiating the Transaction
  • Open your wallet app on your computer or mobile device.
  • Navigate to your USDT balance. This is usually listed under the 'Assets' or 'Wallet' section.
  • Click "Send"
  • Click on the "Send" button. This button is often represented with an upward-pointing arrow or the word "Send."
Step 3: Select "USDT (Tether)"
  • Choose the currency type you wish to send. If a list of cryptocurrencies appears, scroll until you find "USDT" or "Tether."
  • Click on "USDT" to select it as the currency you wish to send.
Step 4: Enter Receiver's Address or Scan QR Code
  • Enter the recipient's address manually in the designated field. Make sure to double-check for any errors. Sending funds to the wrong address usually means they are lost forever. OR Scan the recipient's QR code. If the receiver has provided a QR code, you can scan it using your device's camera to populate the address automatically. This method is less prone to human error.

Confirming the Transaction

After sending, it usually takes up to 30 minutes for the transaction to be confirmed on the blockchain. You can check the status within your wallet or by searching for the transaction ID on a blockchain explorer that supports USDT.

Important Tips

  • Double-Check Addresses: Always double-check the recipient's address. Sending USDT to a wrong address usually results in a loss of funds.

  • Test with Small Amounts: If you are sending a large sum, consider doing a test transaction with a small amount first.

  • Secure Connection: Make sure you are using a secure and reliable internet connection.


By following these steps carefully, you should be able to send USDT without any issues. Always remember to double-check every detail to avoid any mistakes.
